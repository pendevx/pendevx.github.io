Opportunities

Market

The market for VR is currently growing at a rapid pace, which make it an excellent field for development and company growth.
Statistics state that in a 2015 survey, global revenues for VR in the gaming industry was $4.3 billion.
These opportunities brings in a whole set of new gamers brought in to buy their products.

Player

For the player, this new type of technology means there is a newer and fresher field for players to enjoy and try out. The VR details and realisticness provide new experience for players, and this being a quickly expanding field, the semi-immersive and fully-immersive VR would provide a completely new and enriching game environment for the player to discover.

Jobs

With virtual reality being a rapidly developing industry, there are a whole new range of jobs just for this technology. This would bring in new people who have different skills than original video games.
