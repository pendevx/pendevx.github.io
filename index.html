Development of VR Technology in Video Games

VR Technology

In this website you will find different sections giving you information on specific topics involving VR. This site is intended to informa the reader on the societal impacts of VR in video games.

The site is split into 3 main sections. Opportunites based upon the market, the player and the jobs. Risks placed upon the consumer and the seller. Choices based on the economical importance, time saving and for the people.
